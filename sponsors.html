WHY SPONSOR ROBOSOC (NITH)?


Do you care about bringing powerful ROBOTICS programs to Indian youth?
As NIT-H robotics team, we participate in and host ROBOTICS-based competitions involving the design, construction, and operation of robotic technology. Our team stood 25th in ROBOCON 2018. Started in 2015, our students work with professional mentors to operate all aspects of the robotics program from marketing to engineering. This structured environment allows students to take risks, innovate, fail (often), and learn important skills along the way. We are a college-based team and receive limited funding from the college district so we rely on supporters like you. Thank you!
